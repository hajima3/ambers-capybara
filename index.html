Fun Facts

🌍

Capybaras are native to South America and are the largest rodents on Earth!

🏊

They're excellent swimmers with webbed feet and can hold their breath for up to 5 minutes.

🤝

Super social animals! They live in groups of 10-20 and are friends with almost everyone.

😴

Famous for their chill vibes. They're often seen relaxing with birds, monkeys, and other animals.
